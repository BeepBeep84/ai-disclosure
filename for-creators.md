# AI Transparency Levels for Creators

This page is for people who want to use the AI Transparency Levels on their own websites, projects, articles, apps, or repositories.

## How to use the label

Choose the label that most honestly reflects how the final work was created.

These levels are intentionally simple. They are not meant to capture every edge case perfectly. They are meant to make disclosure easier and more normal.

If you are unsure which label fits best, choose the one that best matches the overall level of AI involvement in the final work.

## Guidance

### Choose AI-1 when:
- AI helped with support tasks
- AI was used for proofreading, debugging, checking, or brainstorming
- the main work was still primarily done by a person

### Choose AI-2 when:
- AI helped create meaningful parts of the final work
- a person still directed, reviewed, and edited the result
- the final outcome was shaped through collaboration between a person and AI

### Choose AI-3 when:
- most or all of the final work was generated by AI
- a person mainly reviewed, selected, edited, or published the output

## Notes

This system is about transparency, not quality.

It is not a ranking system.
It is not a moral judgement.
It is not meant to shame anyone for using AI.

It is simply a way to make AI use visible.
